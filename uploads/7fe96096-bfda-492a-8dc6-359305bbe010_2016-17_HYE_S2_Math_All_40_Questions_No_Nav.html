A

Explanation:

Let M = mother's age, D = daughter's age. M + D = 30, (M + 15) = 2(D + 15). From the second equation: M + 15 = 2D + 30, so M = 2D + 15. Substituting: (2D + 15) + D = 30, 3D = 15, D = 5. Therefore M = 25.
